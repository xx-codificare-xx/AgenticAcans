01   About

The Hunter's
Origin Story

So here's how this started. Final year of college, Mumbai. Me and two friends, zero budget, 3am debugging sessions, building an app that used ML to recognise vehicle plates and automate parking. That app got published in AIP Conference Proceedings and won a Best Paper Award. I was completely, irreversibly hooked.

Straight out of college, Ernst & Young picked me up. Two years automating enterprise workflows for some of the biggest organisations in India. Finance. HR. Procurement. Payroll. I improved reporting efficiency by 70%, got promoted from Senior Analyst to Associate Consultant, and walked away with a Client Extraordinaire Award. Then I thought, what's next?

California. I packed up and flew to UC Irvine for my MS in Business Analytics. And I didn't slow down. I'm now building agentic AI at Blue Shield of California that saves 4+ hours a day, in production, not in a deck. Running applied AI research at Merage. Teaching. Mentoring. Still debugging at 3am, just with better tools.

Outside the terminal, I lead as VP of Operations at Women in Business, mentor students as a Teaching Assistant, write poetry under @tryna_ans_me, and yes, I take badminton as seriously as a production outage.
